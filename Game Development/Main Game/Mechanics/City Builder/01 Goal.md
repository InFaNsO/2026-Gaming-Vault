# City Builder — Goal

### Main goal for the player

Grow a **Mani-using civilization** from a single frontier town into a thriving **network of cozy, specialized towns** — and master Mani so completely that you achieve the **Akash Synthesis** (space-time tech), the civilization's crowning achievement.

Concretely, the player:
- **Settles & plans** cozy, household-scale towns (the desirability/planning puzzle — leave room for arriving families).
- **Builds the Mani economy** — harvest raw Mani → refine → power utilities + craft Mani-tech goods that meet escalating household needs/wants.
- **Expands across biomes** — each biome holds a different element, so growth means founding single-species towns (Jal/Agni/Vayu) and weaving the **logistics network** that makes them interdependent.
- **Climbs the eras** — master each element (the tech tree) up the Mani-mastery staircase.
- **Reaches the endgame** — combine all four elements into the **Akash Synthesis** → triggers the endgame sequence.

### What the player is NOT doing

- **Not** fighting — no combat / tower-defense in this game (reserved for the dream game).
- **Not** forced to expand — towns are complete at a comfortable size; growth is available, never demanded (cozy, no treadmill).
- **Not** managing 1,000s of individuals — population is household-granular (a house = a family).
- **Not** running rival/AI colonies — every colony is player-managed; the only "opponent" is your own economy & logistics.

# City Builder — Scope & Depth Allocation (Phase 5)

> **The leash.** City-builders have the genre's highest scope ceiling — left unbounded, the genre eats years. So we commit *in writing* to where depth goes and what is deliberately refused. Principle (locked early): apply the combat method (build a real, complete game) but **concentrate depth on the 4 systems the dream game runs on**, and explicitly bound or cut the rest. Locked 2026-06-07.

---

> **REVISED 2026-06-07** after a depth-allocation review: the game's identity clarified to **"intimate towns, grand network"** (Town-to-City cozy soul + Anno production/logistics brain). Several items moved tiers — town-planning/desirability **elevated to DEEP**; money/services/cim-granularity/terrain moved from CUT to **BOUNDED** (kept in a light, cozy form).

## DEEP — ideal-quality (the dream-game-critical core + the cozy soul)

- **Production-chain economy** — Mani refine → utilities + **Mani-tech goods**; classic×Mani interlock (classic = bodies, Mani = function); **multi-element goods forcing trade**; **building-expansion-via-Mani** (§2.5).
- **Logistics / multi-colony** — 3-tier ladder; abstracted routes w/ map-edge transfer; **Vayu-Mani freight fuel**; network-scale portfolio balancing. *(The system the game most exists to prove for the dream game's Main↔Satellite spine.)*
- **Population tiers & needs** — Anno tiered engine; **1→2→3-element escalation**; Needs + Wants; cheap species bias.
- **Era progression & tech tree** — Mani-mastery staircase; T2C research labs → Knowledge → Akash endpoint.
- **Town planning & desirability (ELEVATED to DEEP 2026-06-07 — the cozy soul):** building **desirability from spatial context** (landscape/elevation proximity, amenities/markets, decoration, adjacency) + **plan-ahead-for-families** at **household granularity** (leave room for arriving families). This is the Town-to-City heart of the game. See `09`.

## BOUNDED — present, competent, deliberately capped (add breadth additively later)

- **Goods / building breadth** — ~5 classic chains, 3-tier goods. NOT Anno's 50-good sprawl.
- **Money / economy** — **Anno model** (passive tax from happy households + trade income from selling surplus − building upkeep) **+ one light tax↔happiness lever** (your "greed vs happiness"). NOT a CS budget-sim with full policy sliders.
- **City services** — police/fire/health etc. as **needs/wants met by proximity** (radius check for now; road-distance = post-prototype revisit), folded into the desirability layer. NOT a crime/fire/disease incident sim.
- **Population granularity** — **household-level** (a house = a family, T2C cozy), NOT 1,000s of independent simulated individuals.
- **Research tree** — single Knowledge currency, element-branched but bounded (per-Mani 4-currency = deferred upgrade).
- **Biome / map content** — **4 biome-maps; each = 1 big home-island town + 1–2 small mining-outpost islands** (DSP model, ~2–4 islands/map); island-procgen *sophistication* deferred to the cross-game procgen discussion.
- **Species** — 4, differentiated by **cheap quantity-bias + ~1 flavor good per tier**, NOT four separate economies.
- **Building expansion** — Mani-gated capacity, few steps per building.
- **Terrain** — **simple verticality / height-tiers** (scenery feeds desirability), grid-compatible.

## CUT — deliberately out (even though an "ideal city game" would have them)

- **Tower-defense / combat** → reserved for the dream game.
- **Environmental Mani-weather** → deferred, revisit post-prototype.
- **Cities:Skylines traffic-agent simulation** → abstracted routes instead.
- **Full CS budget-sim / policy sliders** → only a *light* tax lever kept (see Bounded).
- **City-services *incident* sim** (crime/fire/disease events, coverage modelling) → services are proximity-met needs instead (see Bounded).
- **Individual-agent ("cim") simulation** → population is household-granular (see Bounded).
- **Workshop / modding** → aspiration only, not a target.
- **AI colonies / diplomacy / rival civs** → locked NO.
- **Caves / overhangs / smooth terrain-deform** → only simple height-tiers (see Bounded).

## Optional growth — no forced expansion (LOCKED 2026-06-07)

A town is **fully equipped and viable at a comfortable size.** The game does **NOT require** the player to keep expanding population or chase higher tiers to "win" — players **can** grow as much as they want, but the critical path never demands metropolis-scale. Cozy = no growth-pressure treadmill. (Reinforces the intimate-town identity.)

## Build-order note (depth ≠ build-sequence)

- **Prototype / first-playable = ONE biome (Bhu):** proves the **production + needs + research + building-expansion** loop in a single settlement. Logistics (the deep, dream-game-critical system) is **not** exercised yet (no inter-biome trade in one biome).
- **2nd-biome milestone = logistics comes online** (intra/inter-map routes, Vayu-fuel, portfolio balancing). *This* is where the dream-game-critical logistics proof actually happens.
- Then additional biomes + eras + the Akash endgame layer on **additively.**

## Re-evaluation triggers (when a CUT/BOUND gets revisited)

| Item | Revisit when |
|---|---|
| Environmental Mani-weather | after the prototype |

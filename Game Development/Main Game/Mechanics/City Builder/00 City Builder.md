# City Builder — Overview

> The **City pillar testbed** on the build ladder: a **cozy-magitech Mani-world city builder** — **"intimate towns, grand network"** (Town-to-City's cozy soul + Anno's production/logistics brain), unified by **Mani-as-everyday-infrastructure.** Fresh Mani-world locale, no plot connection (callbacks only). Design pass (Phases 0–6) complete 2026-06-07.

---

## The pitch

You administer a **Mani-using civilization** where Mani is the everyday infrastructure (the electricity & plumbing — Agni stoves, Jal taps, Vayu transport, Bhu construction), spread across a **frontier of biome-islands**. Each biome holds a different element, so you found and grow a **network of cozy, hand-planned towns** — one per biome, each home to one of four species — and weave the **production chains + logistics** that let them depend on each other. Climb the **Mani-mastery eras** to the **Akash Synthesis** (space-time tech) endgame.

## Identity (the through-line)

- **Intimate at the town level** — small, hand-planned, **household-granular** settlements; the joy is the **desirability/planning puzzle**. (Town-to-City soul.)
- **Grand at the network level** — scale comes from the **multi-town production + logistics network**, not city size. (Anno brain.)
- **Cozy** — no combat/TD (reserved for the dream game), no forced growth. Tension = economic/logistics mastery.

## The systems

**DEEP** (why it exists): production-chain economy · logistics / multi-colony · population-tiers & needs · era / tech tree · **town-planning & desirability** (the cozy soul).
**Supporting:** Anno-model money + light tax lever · services as proximity-needs · 4 species (Human/Bhu, Amphibian/Jal, Reptile/Agni, Avian/Vayu) · 4 biome-maps + Akash capstone · **grid substrate + free decoration.**

## Core loop (zoom-in ↔ zoom-out)

`harvest raw Mani → refine (building) → split to utilities + Mani-tech goods → meet household needs/wants → grow tiers → unlock next biome (era) → ship between towns (logistics) → climb to the Akash Synthesis.` Plan each town lovingly (zoom-in); balance the network (zoom-out).

## World callbacks (no plot link)

Mani elements & lore reused from the [[00 Looter Shooter|Looter Shooter]]; **Akash space-time portals = the Breach-echo**; the confident Mani-rich era quietly foreshadows later hubris. **4th species (Avian)** = a "lost species" by the LS era. ⚠ The 4-species count ripples to the dream game.

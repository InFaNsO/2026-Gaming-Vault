
### Game World Brief

- Lithic Mow - A massive drill placed between 2 mountains drilling in the entire valley
	- Used to mine ultra rare [[06 Element in Looter Shooter|Element]] 
- Genesis Vats - A massive series of connected oil rigs complex in a massive lake with hole in the middle releasing vapor that heal minor wounds
	- Vapor and humid conditions is needed to forge [[06 Element in Looter Shooter|Element]]
- Prism Forge - An Array of 5 Massive Glass towers located at the desert banks of the great river overlooking the genesis vats 
	- Uses Lazers and Sun to generate extreme heat that is needed to forge [[06 Element in Looter Shooter|Element]]


### Story Summary

You wakeup in an unknown world from Cryo-Sleep / Ship Crashing. The player has to infeltrate and overtake all other factions and learn what happened that lead to the destruction of society and unlock the secrets to [[06 Element in Looter Shooter|Element]] which will allow the player to rebuild the abandoned [[05 Mega Structures - Game Worlds|Mega Structures]]  


### Main Quest

1. Wake up form Cryo-Sleep
2. Infiltrate and take control of the madmax style society 
3. Fight and take control of mega structures from hostile creatures
4. Develop the Mega Structures to rebuild the society 


### Side Quest

1. Learn about the history of the world
2. Learn about the factions and creatures 
3. Exploit weaknesses of enemies from this knowledge

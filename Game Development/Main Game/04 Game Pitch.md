

A kingdom finds a mystical island 
The king chooses the player to colonize and expand the kingdom to this island

The player is responsible for building a city and exploring the magical dungeon on  the island.

The first time player enters the dungeon and dies he wakes up again at the castle and is sent back again to colonize the island.

expanding into the dungeon will unlock more progression for the player.

their will be dungeon breaks that will occasionally total the city and the player will use remnants to build a new city.


## Game pillars

-> City Building
-> Looter shooter and  Dungeon Crawler
-> Rogue Like Story telling
